Diving Experiences


"SPECIALITY DIVES"



Activity Price 1000/-

The Specialty Dive is a three-dive initiative that offers PADI certified visitors the opportunity to take their diving experience to a whole new level.

Explore the marvels of underwater photography and experience a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity inside the diving cage while our divers feed the Sharks in the aquarium tank.

Dive 1:

Enjoy a dive to remember as you move freely amongst the waters of Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo and come face-to-face with sharks and rays.

Dive 2:

Learn about shark conservation identification, underwater photography tips, and take photos in a 30-minute theory session using either your own or our professional underwater camera and take home memories that will last a lifetime.

Dive 3:

Our Shark Feeding dive is a rare opportunity to interact with these graceful animals during feeding time. Take photos of the Shark Feed from the safety of our diving cage.
