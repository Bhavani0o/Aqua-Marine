Diving Experiences


"SCUBA DIVING"



Activity Price 1000/-

Ever dream what it’s like to breathe underwater in the Aquarium? Do you want to give scuba diving a try but aren’t sure if you’re ready for a full certification course yet?

PADI’s Discover Scuba Diver program is for you. With Al Boom Diving, you can explore the wonder that is Aqua Aquarium.Experience the thrill of Discover Scuba Diving in the spectacular Aqua Marine

Starting at 1:00p.m. 3:00 p.m. or 5:00 p.m.Following a quick theory lesson your Dive Instructor will teach you the basic safety guidelines and skills in the confines of a cage before you venture out for your dive in the 10 million litre Aquarium.
